What is mazha.live?

mazha.live (mazha means "rain" in Malayalam) is a free, community-driven platform that lets anyone in Kerala report and track rainfall in real time. No account needed. No sign-up. Just a pincode and a tap.

Every report appears instantly on a live map, colour-coded by intensity — from drizzle (green) to extreme (red). The result is a district-by-district picture of current rain across Kerala, built by the people who are actually experiencing it.

How It Works

  • 📍
    Enter a Pincode Type any 6-digit Kerala pincode. The app looks up the location instantly using pincode data.
  • 💧
    Select Rain Intensity Choose from five levels — Drizzle, Light, Moderate, Heavy, or Extreme. Each corresponds to a measurable mm/hr range.
  • 🗺️
    See It Live on the Map Your report appears as a coloured dot on the map. The colour and size reflect the intensity, and a heatmap overlay shows overall rainfall density.
  • 🏞️
    Dam Levels & Spillway Alerts Live dam storage percentages and spillway release data are shown alongside the rain map — useful for downstream flood awareness.

⚡ Reports fade over 2 hours — intensity automatically decays until the report expires at 48 hours. Always shows the freshest picture.

Who It's For

  • 🏠
    Kerala Residents Check if it's raining in your area or where you're heading next. See what your neighbours are reporting.
  • ✈️
    Travelers & Commuters Plan around the rain. Check conditions across districts before starting a trip.
  • 🌾
    Farmers Get a sense of recent rainfall in your region. Community-reported data can supplement local observations.
  • ⚠️
    Disaster Preparedness Heavy rain alerts and spillway release information help with situational awareness during monsoon season.

Community-Driven & Always Free

mazha.live is built entirely around crowd-sourced data. Every dot on the map was placed by someone who took a few seconds to report what they were seeing. The more people participate, the more accurate and useful the map becomes.

✅ No account. No ads that trick you. No paywall. The site is supported by non-intrusive Google AdSense ads to cover hosting and development costs.
