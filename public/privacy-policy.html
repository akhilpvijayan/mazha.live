This Privacy Policy explains how mazha.live ("we", "our", or "the Site") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our service. We take your privacy seriously. The platform is designed to work without requiring an account, name, or email address.

What Data We Collect

✓ Information You Voluntarily Submit

  • ✓
    Pincode (PIN code) — A 6-digit Indian postal code you enter to report rain. This is used to determine the approximate location of your report.
  • ✓
    Rain intensity selection — You select a level (Drizzle, Light, Moderate, Heavy, Extreme) corresponding to an estimated mm/hr range. This is displayed publicly on the rain map.
  • ✓
    Timestamp — The time of your report is recorded alongside the PIN and intensity.

✓ Information Collected Automatically

  • ✓
    Approximate geographic location — Derived from the pincode you provide. We do not access GPS or precise device location.
  • ✓
    Browser/device information — Standard web analytics data: browser type, operating system, screen resolution, referrer URL, and pages visited. Collected via Google AdSense and basic server logs.
  • ✓
    IP address — Used in aggregate form for basic analytics and fraud prevention. Not associated with individual rain reports.

What We Do NOT Collect

  • ✗
    No account or registration — You do not need to create an account or sign in to use mazha.live.
  • ✗
    No name or email — We do not ask for your name, email address, phone number, or any personal identifier — unless you choose to contact us directly via email.
  • ✗
    No precise GPS location — We do not access your device's GPS. Location is approximated from the pincode you enter.
  • ✗
    No passwords or credentials — There is no login system, so no passwords, tokens, or authentication data are stored.

How We Use Your Data

🔹 Displaying rain reports — Your PIN, intensity, and timestamp are shown as a colour-coded dot on the public rain map. This is the core function of the service.

🔹 Improving the service — Aggregated, anonymised data helps us understand usage patterns, identify issues, and decide where to focus development.

🔹 Advertising — We use Google AdSense to show non-intrusive ads. AdSense may use cookies and device identifiers as described below.

Cookies & Advertising

mazha.live uses Google AdSense, a third-party advertising service. Google AdSense uses cookies (small text files stored on your browser) and web beacons to serve relevant ads based on your previous visits to this and other websites.

Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to our site and/or other sites on the Internet. You may opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

We do not have access to or control over the cookies used by Google AdSense. You can learn more about Google's privacy practices at Google Privacy & Terms.

🍪 No first-party cookies are set by mazha.live itself (no analytics cookies, no session cookies, no tracking cookies beyond what AdSense delivers).

Data Retention

Rain reports are automatically removed from the active map 48 hours after the last report at that PIN. After 2 hours, reports begin fading in intensity and are marked as "FADED" for another 46 hours before expiring. Aggregate data used for analytics may be retained longer in anonymised form.

Your Rights (GDPR / Data Protection)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA) or the United Kingdom, you have the following rights under applicable data protection law:

  • →
    Right to access — Request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • →
    Right to erasure — Request deletion of your data (note that rain reports are anonymised by PIN and cannot be linked to an individual).
  • →
    Right to object — Object to processing of your data for advertising purposes (opt out via Google Ads Settings).

Since mazha.live does not require accounts or collect names/emails, most personal data rights are exercised through your browser settings and Google's ad preferences. To exercise any of these rights, please contact us.
